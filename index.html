Sigiriya Rock Fortress & Ruins

Step back in time and scale the magnificent Sigiriya Rock Fortress. Often referred to as the "Eighth Wonder of the World", this ancient palace and fortress complex features stunning water gardens, ancient frescoes, and breathtaking panoramic views from its summit. Unveil the rich history of Sri Lankan royalty.

  • Selected route: Cultural Triangle Tour
  • Recommended duration: 1-2 Days
  • 1200 steps to the top
  • World heritage site, perfect for photography
