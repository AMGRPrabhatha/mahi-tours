Our Story

Mahi Maps was born from a passion for sharing the beauty of Sri Lanka with travelers from around the world. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the island's most trusted tourism cab services.

With over a decade of experience, we've helped thousands of travelers discover the hidden gems of Sri Lanka - from the ancient ruins of Anuradhapura to the pristine beaches of Mirissa, from the misty hills of Ella to the wildlife wonders of Yala.

Our mission is simple: provide safe, comfortable, and memorable travel experiences that showcase the true spirit of Sri Lankan hospitality.

How do I book a tour?

Simply navigate to our Tours page, select your preferred package, and click 'Book Now'. You can also contact us directly via WhatsApp or email for custom itineraries.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely! We specialize in tailor-made tours. Let us know your interests, and we'll craft the perfect journey for you.

What is included in the tour cost?

Our tour costs typically include the vehicle, fuel, driver accommodation/meals, and insurance. Entrance tickets and guest meals are usually excluded unless specified.

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes, we provide 24/7 airport pickup and drop-off services. Just share your flight details with us, and our driver will be there to welcome you.

Is it safe to travel in Sri Lanka?

Yes, Sri Lanka is a safe destination for travelers. Our drivers are experienced professionals who prioritize your safety and comfort at all times.
